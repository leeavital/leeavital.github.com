21CC


On Github

21CC (21st Century Cookbook) is a smart cookbook web-app that I worked on during the 2013 MHacks hackathon. It uses neural networks to predict what recipes a user might like.

You start by training on ten sample recipes from our database that we pulled from yummly. After that, you put in some of the ingredients you already have in your pantry. A neural network generates some recipes, which are filtered based on what’s in your pantry. At the end, you get five recipes that you can consequentially rate, which will improve the suggestions even more.

The website is sporadically available at twentyfirstcenturycookbook.
